How we work

Four steps. No surprises.

01

Discover

A working session with our CEO to understand your structure, your books, your appetite for risk, and the decisions you've put off. Nothing is sold here — only mapped.

02

Structure

We propose a configuration of services across the four practices that fits your stage. Pricing is transparent: $5,000 per month per managed entity for recurring work, fixed fees for one-time engagements.

03

Execute

Once you sign, we activate the relevant practice areas. Legal firms, accounting partners, and brokerages are coordinated by us — your team interfaces with one point of contact, not three.

04

Partner

Monthly reviews on financials. Quarterly strategy sessions. Annual P&L analysis with proactive tax recommendations. Decisions made together, executed on your timeline.
